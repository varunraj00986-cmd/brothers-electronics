❓ Frequently Asked Questions

TV repair mein kitna time lagta hai? ▼
Zyada tar repairs same-day complete ho jaati hain. Power board, motherboard jaise complex issues mein 1–2 din lag sakte hain agar part mangwana pade.
Kya aap ghar pe aakar repair karte ho? ▼
Haan! Home visit available hai. Aapko wall-mounted TV bhi utaarna nahi padega. Technician ghar pe aata hai, diagnose karta hai aur wahi repair karta hai jab possible ho.
TV repair par warranty milti hai? ▼
Haan, hum 90-day warranty dete hain har repair par. Agar same problem dobara aaye warranty period mein, toh free revisit aur fix karenge.
TV repair ya naya TV khareedna — kya behtar hai? ▼
Agar TV 5 saal se kam purana hai, toh repair usually sahi rehta hai. Hamare technician aapko honest advice denge inspection ke baad — no forced repairs.
Konse brands repair karte ho? ▼
Samsung, LG, Sony, Panasonic, MI, OnePlus, TCL, Vu, Philips, Hisense, Thomson, Kodak aur sabhi major brands. Agar aapka brand list mein nahi hai, call karke confirm kar lo.
Cracked TV screen repair ho sakti hai? ▼
Cracked screen ko repair nahi kiya ja sakta — sirf replace hoti hai. Hamare technician inspection ke baad replacement ki exact cost batayenge. Panel availability brand aur size par depend karti hai.
Payment kaise karein? ▼
Cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), ya bank transfer — sab accept karte hain. Payment sirf tab karni hai jab aap repair se satisfied ho.
